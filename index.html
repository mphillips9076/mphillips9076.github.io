Why have Greek and Roman myth continuously dominated pop culture?

Myths have constantly circulated in pop culture, whether it be Shakespeare's allusions in his plays, or Rick Riordan's Percy Jackson and the Olympians. In this article, I take a look at why and how ancient mythology has cropped up and what that means for us as a society.

Allie Walsh · Swatting and its Major Impacts

Swatting and Its Major Impacts

The dawn of the internet has spelled increasing desensitization for us as a society. This apathy combined with the anonymity of the internet has led to a very serious online behavior called "swatting," wherein a "swatter" calls in a SWAT team against others in order to harass them. In this podcast, my classmates and I discuss swatting, its impact in the U.S., and how it has progressed over the past few years.

Trying to Make an Ancient Roman Omelet as a College Student

Preserving historical and cultural traditions is vital to preserving our understanding of humanity as a whole -- but you don't have to be a historian to do so, nor do you have to be a major enthusiast! In this video, I try my best to make a cheap and quick ancient Roman omelet while talking about how change can be a good thing for the preservation of traditions.

Save Ancient Studies Alliance

As a Classics student, I naturally believe that all people should have the opportunity to learn about the rich and diverse history of the Ancient Mediterranean. The Save Ancient Studies Alliance also believes in this mission, seeking to increase accessibility and exposure to the ancient world as well as to rethink how the ancient world is studied.
